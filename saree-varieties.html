Saree Varieties

Banarasi Saree

Known for its rich gold and silver zari work, perfect for weddings and festive occasions.

Kanjivaram Saree

A traditional silk saree from South India, renowned for its durability and vibrant colors.

Chanderi Saree

Lightweight with intricate zari and buti work, ideal for daily wear and light events.

Paithani Saree

A luxurious silk saree from Maharashtra with peacock and floral motifs.
